filtering stage defined by the set of inclusion patterns of a query, designed to increase the number of unfulfilled preferences that are expressed by the traditional recall measure by retrieving a set of messages matching a very specific pattern.

filtering stage defined by the set of exclusion patterns of a query, is aimed at reducing unsatisfied preferences due to the inclusion of ``fallout'' messages (messages for which the subscriber would prefer other messages in DB that are not included in the retrieval set).
